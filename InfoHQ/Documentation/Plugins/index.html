Extension

InfoHQ also supports a vary of awesome extensions too, developed, powered and released by One Future Unified Team and the community. All extensions of InfoHQ had been tested and compatible across all releases of InfoHQ.

Comments

InfoHQ have a comment plugin. InfoHQ admin could add this comments plugins onto their InfoHQ site. This plugins will add a comment section onto your InfoHQ pages so everyone could add a comment and discuss about the pages.

Countdown

InfoHQ also have a comment plugin. InfoHQ users and admin could add this countdown plugins onto their InfoHQ sites. This plugins will add a countdown clock into your InfoHQ pages to countdown to a specific time and date.

Translate

InfoHQ also provide a translate plugin. InfoHQ admin could add this translate plugins into their InfoHQ sites for free. This plugins will let users to translate all sites content to their preferenced languages to understand the InfoHQ pages further more using Google Translate API.
