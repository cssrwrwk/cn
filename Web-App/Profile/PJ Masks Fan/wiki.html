    Season 4 is the fourth season of PJ Masks. It was announced on June 5, 2019. It was intended to be set to premiere in April 2020, but it was then moved back to May 15 2020, most likely due to the COVID-19 pandemic. The fourth season majorly focuses on the upgrades of the PJ Masks' original three vehicles. One of the upgrades includes the combination of three vehicles into a new aircraft, the PJ Jet which could go to space. Another vehicle, known as the PJ Sub, is also introduced. This is the first season to have the season premiere on the Disney Junior channel. HQ introduces its basement in this season too. The PJs (with the exception of Owlette and PJ Robot) get their own PJ Wings. This is the first season to have twenty-five episodes although there still were 52 segments. This season introduces Newton Star, Munkigu, full appearance of Octobella, Percival and Pharaoh Boy.
