About Us

In 2100, there are two teenage Chinese twins heroes, named Li Hui and Hu Jie with their parents in Perle out for an adventure to protect their mighty city! Teenager Heroes is written and created by Jst Tan with co-writer Wakjawa, distributed by One Future Unified Team into many various platforms.
