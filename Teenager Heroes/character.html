Characters

Teenager Heroes book series featured many characters across many stories and seasons. Teenager Heroes book series contains many awesome designed character developed by Teenager Heroes team. All of our characters across all of Teenager Heroes stories is listed here:

  • Name: Hu Jie
  • Gender: Male
  • Age: 14
  • Ethnicity: Chinese
  • Occupation: Heroes
  • Description: Hu Jie, a brave and fast hero in Teenager Heroes. The younger brother of Li Hui.
  • Personality:
    • He is brave. He protected his sister when they met trouble and also dared to face naughty villains.
    • He is also fast at doing stuff too but very impatient at waiting.
    • Playful, he could be playful when doing important stuff.
    • Funny, he loves to make everyone else laugh, especially his sister.
  • Weakness:
    • Impatient, he is impatient most of the time and couldn’t wait for others.
  • Power:
    • Speed
    • Running, he could run very fast from location A to location B in a short amount of time. He could run as fast as lightning.
    • Tornado, he could run very fast to create a small tornado inside a small area that will create strong winds.
    • Energy generator, he could generate electric energy using his speed which could restore an out of power building.
  • Gadget:
    • Shield, he could protect himself from attack by enemy and villains by hiding behind his shield.

  • Name: Li Hui
  • Gender: Female
  • Age: 14
  • Ethnicity: Chinese
  • Occupation: Heroes
  • Description: Li Hui, the older sister of Hu Jie. A shy but smart hero in the Teenager Heroes.
  • Personality:
    • Li Hui is smart, she solves any hard problem easily.
    • Li Hui is also shy, she is scared of most people that she meets.
    • Sweet and Careful, she could be careful about taking care of her younger brother.
  • Weakness:
    • Shy, she is scared to face most enemies. She easily gets scared off by scary enemies.
  • Power
    • Technology
      • Computing, she could do some computing like setting up a computer, etc. She could set up a new computer to do stuff that she needs.
      • Hacking, she could hack into other technology infrastructure and make it inaccessible or insecure software.
  • Gadget
    • Bots, she has 2 bots accompanying her at all times that could help to carry stuff, defend her and etc. She could program the bots to do whatever she needs.

  • Name: Min Li
  • Gender: Female
  • Age: 21
  • Ethnicity: Chinese
  • Occupation: Heroes
  • Description: Min Li, the sister of Li Hui and Hu Jie is a hero and university student.
  • Personality:
    • Min Li is smart, she always has a way to solve any trouble they face.
    • Min Li is careful, she always cares for her younger siblings no matter what.
    • Min Li has a good manner too, she is always respectful and polite to everyone and their feelings.
  • Weakness
    • Slow, Min Li is very slow at doing everything, making an easy getaway escape for the villains.
  • Power
    • Creative
      • Drawing, Min Li could draw anything she wanted and make it into a physical product without minutes.
      • Designing, Min Li is very good at designing items. Min Li could design any item she wanted with her power.
  • Gadget
    • Drawing Pen, this is a pen for Min Li to draw anything she wants and make it into reality.

  • Name: Hua Mei
  • Gender: Female
  • Age: 40
  • Ethnicity: Chinese
  • Occupation: Heroes, Scientist
  • Description: The mother of Li Hui, Hu Jie and Min Li, a hero in Perle.
  • Personality:
    • Kindness, Hua Mei has a ton of kindness to her children and everyone else. Hua Mei is always very kind to Min Li, Li Hui and Hu Jie too.
    • Honest, Hua Mei is very honest while doing stuff. She will tell her mistake and apologize for it and would never lie.
    • Intelligent, Hua Mei is very intelligent and smart. She could solve any issues or troubles she faced in any place and anyway.
  • Weakness
    • Vulnerable, Hua Mei is very vulnerable to targets, sickness and often gets exploited unexpectedly.
  • Power
    • Engineering, Hua Mei could create any type of item she wanted.
  • Gadget
    • Solidizer, Hua Mei could print out any type of objects and items that she made to use.

  • Name: Ming Hao
  • Gender: Male
  • Age: 40
  • Ethnicity: Chinese
  • Occupation: Heroes, Scientist
  • Description: The father of the three Chinese siblings, Li Hui, Hu Jie And Min Li, a hero in Perle.
  • Personality:
    • Protective, Ming Hao is very protective of his children, Min Li, Hu Jie and Li Hui. He always protects his children from any troubles and harms.
    • Kind, Ming Hao is very kind to his children and friends. He always uses kind words too.
    • Helpful, Ming Hao is very helpful. Ming Hao always helped with anything he could no matter what.
  • Weakness
    • Forgetful, Ming Hao is very forgetful and always forgets things that he needed.
  • Power
    • Super Strength, Ming Hao has super strength and could punch and lift through anything he wants.
  • Gadget
    • Special Gloves, Ming Hao has special gloves that let Ming Hao use super strength.
