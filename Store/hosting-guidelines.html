One Future Unified Team Guidelines

Welcome to One Future Unified Team Hosting Guidelines! A guidelines for the uses of One Future Unified Team Hosting. We know you hate tons of hard limit so we make this as simple as possible.



Github Pages and Vercel Restriction

We currently using Github and Vercel for website hosting so we are tied to their own hosting terms. You are required to follow their terms on our hosting too. Your site aren't allowed to have get-rich-quick schemes, sexually obscene content, and violent or threatening content or activity.
