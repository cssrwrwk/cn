Welcome to PJ Masks Info!

Welcome to PJ Masks Info InfoHQ! Welcome to PJ Masks Info home pages! An InfoHQ all about Pyjamasques which started on March 29, 2007 by Romuald Racioppo.

Pyjamasques are a few brave heroes character that was created on a French books made by Romuald Racioppo. This few character transformed into heroes in night to save the day from a few naughty villains messing with everyone day and night. The character will face all fear and overcome it teaching everyone moral on how to be a better person.

PJ Masks Info is an InfoHQ all about PJ Masks books series and tv series. A InfoHQ with information of episodes, character, location, facts for entertainment and archived purpose.
