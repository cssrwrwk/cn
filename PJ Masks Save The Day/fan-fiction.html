Fan Fiction Story

OC means an original character. PJ Masks fan might create their own OC for their roleplay and fan fiction story.

PJ Masks Save The Day Admin knows that fan fiction story related to the PJ Masks by the PJ Masks fans existed so we have created a new section in our site specially for this. A place for everyone to upload or read fan fiction story by PJ Masks fans.

If you have a completed fan fiction story, you could submit your fan fiction story to us so it could be uploaded into PJ Masks Save The Day website. We will review each and ALL fan fiction story in a case-by-case basis to ensure it follow our community guidelines. You could upload via the Google Form here.
