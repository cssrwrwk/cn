PJ Masks: Power Heroes

Hello everyone! I am Jst Tan. Today, I would like to announce a good news, the official announcement of PJ Masks Season 6, from Annecy International Animation Film Festival!

PJ Masks Season 6 will has a new name, PJ Masks: Power Heroes and a new logo too. PJ Masks: Power Heroes will feature preschool icons Catboy, Gekko and Owlette as they are joined by an expanded crime-fighting team to take on even bigger challenges in an all-new series.

PJ Masks: Power Heroes also introduces a brand new aspects such as locations, specifically a new planet and a frozen wilderness, and characters. PJ Masks: Power Heroes will also features more specials, specifically 3 44-minute episodes and 6 half-hour episodes.

PJ Masks: Power Heroes is slated to premiere in 2023. It was first confirmed officially in June 18 2022, although it was early revealed to some back in April & May 2022.

That is all about PJ Masks: Power Heroes we know so far but more information will be released soon. Stay tuned! I had nothing more to said so see you in the next blog post. I hope you had a nice time and let be patient while waiting for PJ Masks: Power Heroes. Bye for now.
